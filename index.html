About AR Rental & Sales

We bring the ability for sellers to allow buyers to view potential new properties in the comfort of their home.

We do this by providing a VR experience to buyers to their phones and laptops. They can view any property at anytime with no hassle. No need to set up an appointment or contact an agent.

Testimonies

Retailers

Number of testimonies: 6, found through Zillow and contacted through call

  • When houses are priced below 200,000, It's a sellers market where customers are having trouble viewing the house before being sold
  • When they are priced at 300,00+, It's a buyers market where houses sit on the market for longer periods of time
  • All 6 Retailers agreed virtual reality tours would be helpful to them, It saves them time by only bringing them serious buyers
  • 3 have used some sort of VR such as video and Matterport, Problems with these technologies is getting a true perspective of a property, or using the technology themselves.
  • Prices for pictures are $135. Video VR is $250. 3 Retailers are willing to pay $300-400 while the other 3 are willing to pay $200-250.
